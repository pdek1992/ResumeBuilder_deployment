# VigilSiddhi AI Resume Builder & Job Application Suite

The world's most advanced AI-powered career success platform. Built with Next.js, Supabase, and a multi-provider AI engine.

## 🚀 Ultimate Feature Suite

### 🧠 Core AI Capabilities
- **AI-Powered Resume Tailoring**: Real-time analysis of resumes against Job Descriptions (JD) with dynamic content generation.
- **Conversational Experience Discovery**: Chat with VigilSiddhi AI to uncover hidden skills and undocumented achievements.
- **Truth-Preserving Optimization**: Smart reframing of existing experiences to match JD keywords without fabrication.
- **Real-time ATS Scoring**: Live 0-100 compatibility scores with actionable feedback roadmaps.

### 📄 Comprehensive Content Generation
- **Dynamic Cover Letters**: Personalized letters generated from your resume and specific job roles.
- **Interview Prep Suite**: Targeted mock interview questions, STAR story guidance, and tone analysis.
- **Achievement Optimizer**: Transform dry job duties into high-impact, quantifiable bullets.

### 🎨 Advanced Resume Building
- **Premium Templates**: Multiple ATS-optimized and creative layouts (Modern, Swiss, Material).
- **Full Customization**: Accent colors, typography (Google Fonts), margins, and section management.
- **Multi-Resume Management**: Create, duplicate, lock, and manage multiple versions of your professional profile.

### 🌍 Global & Technical Excellence
- **Multi-Provider AI**: Support for Google Gemini, OpenAI, and Anthropic with custom user-provided API keys.
- **High-Fidelity Exports**: Export to professional PDF (A4/Letter) and DOCX formats.
- **PWA Ready**: Installable on desktop and mobile for offline access.
- **Security First**: Multi-Factor Authentication (TOTP) and Passkey (WebAuthn) support.

---

## 🛠 User Workflow

1. **Onboarding**: Create an account or sign in securely with MFA/Passkeys.
2. **Import**: Upload an existing PDF/DOCX or start from a template. AI extracts your data automatically.
3. **Targeting**: Add a Job Description and Company name to the "Targeting" section.
4. **Tailor**: Use "AI Tailor" to optimize your summary and highlights for the specific role.
5. **Chat**: Use the ✨ **AI Chat Sidebar** to request complex edits (e.g., "Add a project about React-Native" or "Make my role at Google sound more leadership-oriented").
6. **Analyze**: Check the real-time ATS score and follow the suggestions to hit 90%+.
7. **Generate Suite**: Generate a matching Cover Letter and Mock Interview preparation materials.
8. **Export**: Download your high-fidelity resume in PDF or DOCX format.
9. **Secure**: Lock your resume to prevent accidental edits once finalized.

## ⚙️ Technical Stack
- **Frontend**: Next.js (App Router), Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion.
- **Backend**: Supabase (Auth, Database, Edge Functions).
- **AI Engine**: Custom service supporting Gemini 1.5 Pro, GPT-4o, and Claude 3.5.
- **Payments**: Razorpay secure integration for premium features.
- **Deployment**: Vercel.

## 🛡 Security & Privacy
